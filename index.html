About Us

PlacementCentral is an all in one placement preparation portal where you can learn and solve a variety of questions .PlacementCentral gives you quizzes to practice , learn and evaluate your c, c++ , Java skills and a lot more general quizzes to go through your entire course . One can download company wise written question papers as well as answers . PlacementCentral also provides with a large number of interview questions asked in variety of companies ranging from Microsoft ,Google, Facebook, Amazon , Adobe and many more. And that is not all , PlacementCentral's Placement Predictor predicts the companies you could be placed in the near future based on your resume and skills. This really helps you get an idea of how you could improve and work toward getting your dream company .
